Wrapped always delivers an outstanding service.

We believe planning and logistics are key components to a successful shoot. Our process allows you to focus on the creative while we focus on everything else.

Within our structure we manage a broad range of services, which include detailed and cost effective budget management, model casting, talent booking and location scouting for both still and motion shoots. Wrapped clients can also benefit from our professional relationships when it comes to fee and usage negotiation.

Regardless of budget and scale of the shoot, Wrapped provides a unique and focused service to all clients. Depending on your requirements we can provide as much or as little assistance as you require.
